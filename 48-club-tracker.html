Track your journey to see a match involving every team in Iceland's top four divisions, at their permanent home ground.

How it works: The 48 Club is inspired by England's 92 Club. You must attend a match at each team's home ground—and since some teams share a ground (like Breiðablik, HK, and Kári at Kópavogsvöllur), you need to tick off each team, not just each venue. Click a team card to mark it visited. Your progress saves automatically.
